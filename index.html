About us

It is a prestigious food and agriculture event curated to showcase current trends and technological advancements in the food and agriculture industry. It is one of the biggest exhibitions in the food, agriculture, and home appliances sectors in Osmanabad/Dharashiv.

Event Profile

Khadya – Kisan 2024 is a useful event for exhibitors and visitors alike, scheduled to be held from March 7th to March 10th, 2024, in Osmanabad/Dharshiv. More than 50 exhibitors are set to represent and showcase a vast range of food, agricultural products, home appliances, agricultural equipment, etc.
